The Story Behind the Song

A Kid, a Bridge, and a Saltwater Town

Summer 1987. Craig Whitaker was just a kid wedged in the middle seat of a minivan, holding his breath as the family crossed the rickety old bridge into Wildwood, New Jersey. The AC went off. The windows went down. And the whole world smelled like the ocean.

"Better Than I Remember" isn't just a song — it's a postcard from a time when life was simple, summers were infinite, and the boardwalk felt like the center of the universe. Hunt's Pier. Keystone Cops. Carnival rides and Kohr Bros.

Decades later, Craig still finds himself on those same streets — now with his own family. And somehow, impossibly, it's all even better than he remembers.

Wildwood, NJ — circa 1985

A Collaboration Born for the Shore

Introducing Blue Claw
Lemon Lager

When Craig Whitaker sat down with the team at King's Road Brewing Company, they had one question: what does a perfect Shore summer taste like in a can?

The answer is Blue Claw Lemon Lager — bright, crisp, and finished with a splash of refreshing lemon. Brewed for long weekends, live music, and raising a can with friends while the band plays on.

Inspired by the song "Better Than I Remember."

From the Brewery

When King's Road Brewing Company teams up with Craig Whitaker of Blue Claw County, you know it's going to taste — and sound — like summer at the Shore. Inspired by Craig's song "Better Than I Remember," our new Blue Claw Lemon Lager captures that same easygoing nostalgia: sunshine on your face, salt in the air, and nights that somehow turn out even better than you expected.

Bright, crisp, and finished with a splash of refreshing lemon, Blue Claw Lemon Lager is brewed for long weekends, live music, and raising a can with friends while the band plays on. Just like the song, it's smooth, memorable, and made for moments you'll want to relive — because sometimes the second sip really is better than you remember."

— Bob Hochgertel, Co-Owner and Managing Director, King's Road Brewing Company
